Founding President's message

A cultural ambition for the automobile in Morocco.

M. Abdellah Abdellaoui presents the Museum as a meeting point for enthusiasts, professionals, education and the transmission of automotive culture.

Abdellah Abdellaoui · Founding President

A crossroads for enthusiasts and automotive professionals.

This project is part of the momentum initiated by His Majesty the King, who elevated the arts and culture to essential pillars of national development. Morocco's ambition to become one of the world's most influential automotive hubs calls for a strong culture of the automobile to be shared across the entire ecosystem.

The Museum of the Automobile of Morocco was conceived as a point of convergence for enthusiasts and professionals alike, offering a space where knowledge and passion for the automobile are not only celebrated but actively shared.

We see this place as a leading cultural and tourist destination, a dynamic platform for exchanges and encounters in the automotive world. We have enriched it with an Automotive Academy and a national museum, both dedicated to training and to passing on the history of the automobile.

My team and I reaffirm our commitment to excellence and innovation at the heart of the automotive ecosystem.
